Features

Everything you need
in the palm of your hand

From the collaborative map to GPS driver mode — Alertoo has what matters for people on the road.

🗺️

Real-Time Map

Color-coded pins by category — DUI checkpoint, accident, flood, roadwork. Understand traffic in 1 second.

🚦

Report in 1 Tap

Report a checkpoint or accident in seconds. Your location is only used while the app is open — never in the background.

🎉

Events near you

Parties, concerts, bars, restaurants and festivals appear on the map. Discover what's happening in your city today.

🧭

GPS Navigation

Driver mode with TTS (voice alerts), alerts along your route and turn-by-turn navigation integrated with Google Maps and Waze.

🔔

Smart Notifications

Get alerts when an event appears within 5 km of you. Save routes and be notified of events along your commute.

🏆

Ranking & Achievements

Earn points by reporting events and unlock badges. See your rank on your city's leaderboard.

How it works

That simple.

No complicated sign-up, no background tracking. Just you and the road.

1

Open the map

When you open the app, the map automatically loads events near your location. No configuration needed.

2

Tap to report

Tap the map → choose the event type → confirm. In less than 10 seconds the alert appears for all nearby users.

3

Earn points and help the community

Each report confirmed by the community earns points. Climb the ranking, unlock badges and become your city's reference.
